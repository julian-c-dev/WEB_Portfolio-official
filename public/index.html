About Me

Since I had my first PC in 2003, I have always been interested in computers and Web development; despite the fact that my professional career took a different path (History and Tourism).

Due to the pandemic, I was not able to work in those fields for a while, giving me a wonderful opportunity to turn something that I always did for fun into something more, a new career, a new purpose in life.

Although I know I am at the beginning of a path that will last forever, I know I will walk along with a smile on my face.
