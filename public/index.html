About

Hello! My name is Julian and I enjoy building components for Websites and desktop applications.

Since I bought my first PC in 2003, I have always been interested in web development, despite the fact that my professional career took a different path.

Due to the pandemic, I am no longer able to work in my field, giving me a wonderful opportunity to turn something that I always did for fun into something more, a new career, a new purpose in life.

In September 2020, I began learning all of the Front-End technologies I had always wanted to learn on a professional level. It seems like a short period of time, but my entire time has been dedicated to this purpose, and I plan to keep updating my skills.

Although I know I am at the beginning of a path that will last forever, I know I will walk along with a smile on my face.
